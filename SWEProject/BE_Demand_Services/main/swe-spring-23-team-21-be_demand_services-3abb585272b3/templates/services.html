Lab Sample Delivery

We are revolutionizing the way medical facilities transport biological samples and specimens with our autonomous vehicle service. Lab Sample Delivery offers closed and controlled environment reducing the risk of contamination and increasing efficiency. This service is for Lab Tech employees who work with the transporation of specimine for patient care.

Benefits and Features of Lab Sample Delivery:

  • Autonomous vehicle transportation for biological samples and specimens.
  • Closed and controlled environment for reduced contamination and increased accuracy and reliability of medical test results.
  • On-demand scheduling, automated tracking, and cost-effective service for increased efficiency and security.
  • Improve patient care by reducing wait times, and providing optimal transportation conditions
